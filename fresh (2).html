GO FRESH

GO FRESH starts with farm-fresh, organic fruits and vegetables to build its easy on-the-go soups, bowls, and smoothies. The company works directly with farmers to harvest the best crop at the peak of freshness, then freezes it right on the farm to preserve the nutrients of each ingredient. The meals and bowls are pre-portioned and ready in minutes, making this the perfect solution for someone who needs nourishing snacks and lunches on the go. Plus, all of GO FRESH'S selections are 100 percent plant-based and vegan, as well as packaged in 100 percent compostable & recyclable materials that are made from plant-based, renewable fiber. You don't have to choose between preserving the planet or preserving yourself.
